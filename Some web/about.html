About SES JKUAT

Empowering Future Engineers Since 1994

Our History

Founded in 1994, the Society of Engineering Students (SES) JKUAT Chapter has been at the forefront of engineering education and student development. What started as a small group of passionate engineering students has grown into a vibrant community of over 1000 members.

1994

Establishment

SES JKUAT was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical engineering applications.

2005

First Technical Symposium

Organized our inaugural engineering symposium, bringing together industry experts and students.

2015

Innovation Hub Launch

Established our innovation hub to support student projects and startups.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To foster professional development, leadership skills, and technical expertise among engineering students through practical experiences, networking, and community service.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering student society in East Africa, producing well-rounded engineers who are ready to solve global challenges through innovation and sustainable solutions.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Fostering creative thinking and novel solutions to engineering challenges.

Collaboration

Working together to achieve common goals and share knowledge.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in everything we do.

Service

Giving back to society through engineering solutions.
